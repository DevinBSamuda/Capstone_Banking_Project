Services Provided in the Application

These services were created one by one during the journey of creating the application. As each service was added, the application needed upgrades such as Node JS, Sequelize, and MySQL. This process required rewriting the services to best suit the additional assistance.

Sign Up

The purpose of signing up is to create a personal account on a website or app, enabling users to access its features, customize their profile, and receive personalized experiences.

Sign in

Signing in authenticates and authorizes a user to access a system or service, providing a personalized experience and secure storage of user data.

Forgot Password

To enable users to reset their password in case they forget it or need to change it for security reasons. It is a common feature on websites and apps to help users regain access to their accounts.

Withdrawal

Coding for withdrawing cash enables users to securely and efficiently access their funds by interfacing with a financial institution's API to initiate the transaction and update the account balance accordingly.

Balance

To display the balance, the users are provided a way to view their account balance in a clear and accessible format. It helps users monitor their account activity and make informed financial decisions.

Deposit

The purpose of depositing cash is to add money to a bank account or financial institution for safekeeping, future use, and electronic transactions. It is a common practice for managing personal or business finances.

After providing the various services our application offers, it is important to ensure that these services run smoothly and without interruption. To achieve this, we implement error handling techniques that anticipate and manage potential errors that may occur during the use of our services.
